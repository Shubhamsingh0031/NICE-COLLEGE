Course on Computer Concepts (CCC)

Learn the basics of computers and become digitally literate.

About the Course

CCC is a short-term certification course designed to impart basic computer knowledge to beginners. It helps students, professionals, and common citizens understand the fundamentals of computers and IT applications.

Course Details

Duration: 3 Months

Eligibility: Open to all (No prior computer knowledge required)

Why Choose CCC?

  • Learn basic computer operations
  • Understand MS Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Get knowledge of Internet & Email usage
  • Improve digital literacy for jobs & exams

Subjects / Curriculum

  • Introduction to Computers
  • Operating Systems (Windows/Linux)
  • MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint
  • Internet, Social Media, Email
  • Digital Payments & Online Services
  • Cyber Security Basics
