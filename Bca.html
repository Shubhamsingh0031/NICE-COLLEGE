Launch your tech career with NICE COLLEGE's future-ready BCA program.

About the Course

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) at NICE COLLEGE is a 3-year undergraduate program (6 semesters) designed to provide strong foundations in programming, databases, web & mobile development, networking, cloud, and data science. Students graduate with hands-on projects and career-ready skills.

Program Highlights

20+ Computer Labs & 30+ Projects
Industry Exposure: Guest talks & internships
Career Support: Resume clinics & mock interviews
Active Coding & Tech Clubs

Curriculum Snapshot

  • Semester I: Fundamentals of IT, C Programming, Digital Electronics, Mathematics, Communication Skills
  • Semester II: Data Structures, OOP (C++), DBMS, Mathematics II, Environmental Studies
  • Semester III: Computer Networks, Operating Systems, Web Technologies, Java, Minor Project
  • Semester IV: Software Engineering, Python, Advanced DBMS, Mobile App Basics, Minor Project II
  • Semester V: Cloud & DevOps, Data Science, Elective (AI/ML | Cybersecurity | IoT), React/Django, Soft Skills
  • Semester VI: Elective II (Blockchain | Big Data | Testing), Entrepreneurship, Major Project, Internship

Eligibility & Admissions

  • 10+2 (any stream) with Mathematics preferred
  • Admissions based on merit / counseling
  • Scholarships available for eligible students

Documents Required: Mark-sheets, ID, photos, transfer certificate.
