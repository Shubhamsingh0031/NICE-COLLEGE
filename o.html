O-Level (DOEACC / NIELIT Certification)

About the Course

The O-Level Certification by NIELIT (DOEACC) is a foundation-level computer course. It is designed to provide students with basic knowledge of Information Technology, programming, networking, and the Internet. This certification is highly valued for entry-level jobs in IT and government sectors.

Course Details

Duration: 1 Year (2 Semesters)

Eligibility: 10+2 Pass or ITI / Polytechnic diploma
