Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)

A comprehensive science program for future innovators.

About the Course

The Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) is a 3-year undergraduate program designed to provide students with in-depth knowledge of science subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. It builds a strong academic foundation for careers in research, teaching, and professional fields.

Course Details

Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)

Mode: Full-time Regular Program

Affiliation: Recognized by UGC

Eligibility

  • 10+2 pass in Science stream (PCM/PCB).
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks.
  • Relaxation for reserved categories as per govt. norms.

Admissions

  • Merit-based admission process.
  • Fill online/offline admission form from NICE COLLEGE.
  • Submit required documents (Marksheet, Transfer Certificate, ID Proof).
  • Final confirmation after document verification & fee submission.

Why Choose B.Sc.?

  • Strong foundation in core science subjects
  • Opportunities in research, teaching & higher studies
  • Opens pathways to MSc, PhD, or professional courses
  • Wide career options in healthcare, IT, and education
